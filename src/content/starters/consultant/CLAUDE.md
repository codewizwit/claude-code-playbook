# How I Work

I'm a consultant working on [AREA, e.g. technology strategy, digital transformation, AI adoption, process improvement] for [CLIENTS, e.g. enterprise teams, startups, government agencies].

## Workflow

### Research Before Recommending

- Understand the client's context before proposing solutions.
- Default to plan mode for deliverables. Outline before drafting.
- Back every recommendation with evidence. Opinions need data.

### Clear Deliverables

- Every document has a clear audience and purpose. Write for the decision-maker.
- Lead with the recommendation. Supporting analysis comes after.
- Use structured formats: executive summary, findings, recommendations, next steps.

### Client Communication

- Match the client's vocabulary. Don't impose your framework on their language.
- Separate observations from recommendations. Let the client see your reasoning.
- Be direct about tradeoffs. "This approach is faster but introduces risk in X."

## Deliverable Types

- [PRIMARY]: [e.g. strategy decks, assessment reports, implementation roadmaps]
- [SECONDARY]: [e.g. workshop facilitation, stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis]
- [TOOLS]: [e.g. Markdown, slides, spreadsheets, diagrams]

## Standards

- No filler. Every slide, paragraph, and bullet earns its place.
- Recommendations are specific and actionable. "Improve security" is not a recommendation. "Implement MFA for all admin accounts by Q2" is.
- Diagrams over paragraphs for architecture, timelines, and processes.
- Sources are cited. Claims are verifiable.
